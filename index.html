Overall Launch Readiness: 4.8 / 10 -- Entity formed, founder agreements done, formulation locked. Critical gaps: no manufacturer contract signed, no FDA food facility registration, no Nutrition Facts panel, no process authority letter, no fulfillment setup. 6-8 months of execution needed before launch.

Top 5 Critical Gaps (Block Launch)

1. No manufacturing contract signed -- COSMAX or alternative not finalized. 120K minimum per SKU = $150K+ commitment. Must happen ASAP.
2. No process authority letter / thermal processing validation for shelf-stable pouch. FDA requires this for low-acid/acidified foods.
3. No FDA food facility registration (21 CFR 1.225). Required BEFORE manufacturing begins. Both US importer and Korean manufacturer need registration.
4. No Nutrition Facts panel designed or verified. Requires lab analysis (proximate analysis) of final production formula. Not Supplement Facts.
5. No fulfillment / 3PL partner selected for US distribution. Shelf-stable pouches need ambient warehousing but still need logistics.

Key Insight

Pheeew owns the only empty cell that matters: Synbiotic + Pouch/Shot format. No other brand combines prebiotic fiber AND probiotics in a shelf-stable, single-serve, ready-to-drink pouch. GoodBelly is the closest (synbiotic + RTD) but they're in decline, never owned the "synbiotic" term, and use cartons/bottles (not pouches). This is a category of one.
